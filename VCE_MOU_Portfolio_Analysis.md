## 📊 Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive analysis of Victory Child Empowerment (VCE) Kenya's portfolio of five Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) with international partners. The analysis examines critical challenges in funding, capacity management, and organizational sustainability.

### Key Findings

- **5 Active MOUs** with international partners
- **$16,500** in disclosed funding vs. **$32,250-$53,150** estimated true cost
- **51-69% funding gap** creating organizational sustainability risk
- **162-250% capacity utilization** - severe overallocation
- **6 critical risks** requiring immediate intervention

## 🎯 Key Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (0-7 Days)
1. **Freeze portfolio growth** - No new commitments
2. **Emergency renegotiations** - Child's i Foundation (timeline) and Shelter Yetu (budget)
3. **Establish Project Management Office** - Centralized coordination
4. **Create transparency dashboard** - Visible to all partners
5. **Define minimum viable metrics** - 3-5 indicators per partnership

### Critical Interventions Required
- Implement full-cost recovery pricing (100% direct costs + 15-20% overhead)
- Establish independent fund management oversight
- Develop standardized MOU templates with safeguards
- Build basic MEL system
- Create capacity utilization tracking
